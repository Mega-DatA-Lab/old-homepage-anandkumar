The MEGA DatA Lab focuses on developing novel techniques and analyses for model estimation via graphical approaches, and applying them for analyzing high-dimensional data. In the theoretical domain, currently, we are investigating graph estimation in probabilistic graphical models and parameter estimation in latent variable models. We are applying these methods to exciting problems in social network analysis, computational biology and text analysis.
